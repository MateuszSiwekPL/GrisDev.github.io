I always had keen interest in game industry. When I was a kid I always told everyone that I will become game developer when I grow up. Those plans changed when my parents pushed me to study Environmental Engineering because it was our family profession. After completing those studies, I decided that I don’t have enough interest in this field to continue studying it, so I started Master studies in Health and Safety. Those were better suit for me but I still felt like I want to do something different in life.

During this time I picked programming as a hobby. At first I was learning HTML, CSS and JavaScript by creating some fun and basic sites and applications. Then I started learning C# and Unity, to check if game development still interested me.

I realized that, this is what I wanted to do in life so I started looking at game dev more seriously than just as a hobby. I spend roughly half of my free time reading, watching and learning everything about: Unity, C# and game development. I used Unity Learn for courses on programming and Unity tutorials. I also loved watching YouTube videos about different game mechanics and how to create them.

Half a year ago I started making my own games. Right now I developed and published 4 mobile games and I’m currently creating PC multiplayer FPS game that hopefully will be released by the end of July. During this month I will also finish my Master Studies and will be able to finally start looking for a job or internship in game development that till now was only a hobby and dream.
